15. Dia perempuan cantik. positif negatif intorogativenya​


Jawaban:

Positive/Affirmative sentences

She has a beautiful girl

Negative sentences

She has not a beautiful girl

Interogative sentences

Has she a beautiful girl

Penjelasan:

Positive/Affirmative sentences

"Has" is used for subject she, he, it

"Have" is used for subject they, we, I , You

S + Has/Have + V3

Example/contoh: She has gone to Surabaya

They have studied English

I have eaten a plate of rice

Negative sentences

We add "not" after have/has

S + Has not/have not + V3

Example/contoh: Dewi has not arrived yet

Amar has not drunk the coffe

Interogative sentences

Has/have +S + V3

Example/contoh: •Has she walked on the

pavement?

• Have you said the matter to

your mother?

• Has Aisyah pray in

Baiturrohman mosque?

Penjelasan:

rumus nya :

(+) S + be (Am, Is, Are) + adj

➡she is beautiful

(-) S + be (Am, Is, Are) + not + adj

➡she is not beautiful.

(?) be (Am, Is, Are) + S + adj

➡is she beautiful?

keterangan : (+) ➡ positive

(-) ➡ negative

(?) ➡ interrogative
