5. deskripsi valentino rossi


Valentino Rossi or The Doctor is a very successful MotoGP rider. He was born in Urbino, Italy on February 16th, 1979. His mother is Stefania Palma and his father is Graziano Rossi.

Since childhood, Rossi already liked the racing world. It was not separated from his father who is a former 250cc GP rider and has been the third prize at the World Championship in Circuit Racing Morbidelli.

Valentino Rossi had won the world championship title in a row in four different classes, namely in the 125cc class champion, once in the 250cc class, and seven times in the top class, 500cc and MotoGP. Now, Rossi is also widely known as one of the legends of the sport in the early 21st century.

Besides love of racing, Rossi was also crazy about football. He was one of the fans of Inter Milan. Rossi often watched the Inter Milan’s match.

Rossi was also known close friends with Materazzi and Balotelli. In fact, in the German Grand Prix in 2006 Rossi celebrated his winning by wearing shirts of the Italian national team numbered 23, belonged to Materazzi.
