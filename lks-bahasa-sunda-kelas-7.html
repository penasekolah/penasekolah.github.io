29. Bisa kerjakan, lks kelas 7 halaman 24bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

1. The mother always thinks of Her children, before thinking about Herself

2. They have a son. His name is James.

3. The cat cleans its body by using its tongue.

4. This bag is not mine. This bag is hers.

5. The story is boring. Other people have nothing to do with it.

6. We are camping at the campsite now. We need to cook for food here.

7. Take care of her even though you are busy.

8. Sometimes, They can be so selfish. She only thinks about Themselves.

9. She shares hers (her sandwich) with her friends.

10. My father bought a bicycle for me yesterday. This white bicycle is mine.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Pronouns (kata ganti) utamanya Subject Pronouns (kata ganti subjek), Object Pronouns (kata ganti objek), Possessive Pronouns (kata ganti kepemilikan), Possessive Adjective (kata sifat kepemilikan) dan Reflexive Pronouns (kata ganti refleksif) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Subject Pronouns: I, you, he, she, we, they

Object Pronouns: me, you, him, her, us, them

Possessive Pronouns: mine, ours, yours, his, hers, its, and theirs

Possessive Adjectives: my, our, your, his, her, its, and their

Reflexive Pronouns: myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves and themselves.
