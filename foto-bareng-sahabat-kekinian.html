= [tex]\frac{22}{7}[/tex] × 10,5 × 10,5

= [tex]\frac{22}{\cancel{7}}[/tex] × [tex]\sf\cancel{10,5}[/tex] × 10,5

= 22 × 1,5 × 10,5

= 33 × 10,5

= 346,5 cm²

_

K = 2 × π × r

= 2 × [tex]\frac{22}{7}[/tex] × 10,5

= [tex]\sf\frac{22}{7}[/tex] × 21

= [tex]\sf\frac{22}{\cancel{7}}[/tex] × [tex]\sf\cancel{21}[/tex]

= 22 × 3

= 66 cm

19. [50]Permutasi kata:- Foto- Sama- Gunung- Jelahjangan ngasalnote:Tadi rame bareng.. sekarang sepi bareng.. _-​


》Kaidah Pencacahan

FOTO

F = 1 O = 2 T = 1

Jumlah huruf = 4

Huruf ganda = O = 2

= Jumlah huruf! / Huruf ganda!

= 4! / 2!

= 4 × 3 × 2! / 2!

= 4 × 3

= 12 susunan

SAMA

S = 1 A = 2 M = 1

Jumlah huruf = 4

Huruf ganda = A = 2

= Jumlah huruf! / Huruf ganda!

= 4! / 2!

= 4 × 3 × 2! / 2!

= 4 × 3

= 12 susunan

GUNUNG

G = 2 U = 2 N = 2

Jumlah huruf = 6

Huruf ganda = G = 2 U = 2 N = 2

= Jumlah huruf! / Huruf ganda!

= 6! / 2! 2! 2!

= 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 × 2! / 2! × 4

= 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 / 4

= 360 / 4

= 90 susunan

JELAH

J = 1 E = 1 L = 1 A = 1 H = 1

Jumlah huruf = 5

Huruf ganda = -

= Jumlah huruf!

= 5!

= 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

= 120 susunan
