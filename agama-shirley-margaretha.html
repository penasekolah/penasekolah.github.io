The question above inidcate us to fill the blank so the conversation becomes proper dialogue. To fill in the blank, we need to undestand the meaning in the conversation. The answer to fill in the blank are (1) Hi ! ; (2) Hi! ; (3) Bye-bye ; (4) See you tomorrow.

Pembahasan :

In the blank dialogue above, we need to fill the blank with greeting and farewell phrase. Greeting phrase is a word is used when we meet someone or we need to open a conversation. Examples for greeting phrase are Hi!, Hello, how are you ? How do you do, etc. Farewell phrase is a word that we use when we need to leave someone or need to leave a conversation. Examples for farewell phrase are bye-bye, I need to go, see you later,see you tomorrow, etc.

After understanding the meening between greating and farewell phrase we can answer the dialogue above.

12. Roy, shirley and tim shared a sum of money between them in the ratio 4:9:11. shirley had $100 more than roy. how much money did tim have?


Jawaban:

Let's assume the total sum of money they shared is $x.

According to the ratio, Roy received 4x/24 of the total sum, Shirley received 9x/24, and Tim received 11x/24.

Shirley had $100 more than Roy, so Roy received 4x/24 - $100.

So, the total amount of money received by Roy and Shirley is 4x/24 - $100 + 9x/24 = 13x/24 - $100.

Since they shared the whole sum of money, we can set the equation:

x = 13x/24 - $100 + 11x/24

24x = 24 * (13x/24 - $100) + 24 * (11x/24)

24x = 13x - $2400 + 11x

24x = 24x - $2400

$2400 = 0

Therefore, Tim had $11x/24 of the total sum, which is $11x/24 = $0.

17. shirley suggests a new idea uhah kebentuk present perfect ​


Shirley suggest a new idea.

Formula present perfect =

subject + has/have + past participle.

Penjelasan :

Has digunakan untuk singular

Have digunakan untuk plural

Shirley -> singular

Jadi yg digunakan has.

Past pasticiple-nya suggest = suggested

Jawaban :

Shirley has suggested a new idea.

Formula present perfect continuous :

Subject + has/have + been + verb + ing.

Penjelasan :

Shirley -> singular

Jadi yg digunakan has.

Verbnya di kalimat ini adalah suggest.

Jawaban :

Shirley has been suggesting a new idea.

29. Margaretha berjalan kaki dengan kecepatan rata-rata 3 m/detik. Berapakah jarak yang ditempuh olehnya setelah 1 jam berjalan?​


Jawaban:

3.600 m.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

kecepatan rata ratanya 3 m/detik.

1 jam = 3.600 detik

= 3 m/detik × 3.600 detik

= 3.600 m.

jadi jarak yang ditempuh olehnya setelah 1 jam berjalan adalah 3.600 m.
