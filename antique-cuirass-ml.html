24. that antique car will be bought by my father. the active form is


Jawaban:

My father will buy that antique car.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat pasif dengan menggunakan modal verb (will, can, might, may dll) dibentuk dengan bantuan auxiliary verb (kata kerja penolong)  be dan mengubah verb-nya menjadi verb 3.

Tentunya tidak lupa mengganti posisi objek di kalimat aktif menjadi subjek di kalimat pasif dan sebaliknya jika mengubah kalimat [asif ke kalimat aktif.

Dalam soal di atas:

That antique car will be bought by my father.

(Mobil antik itu akan dibeli oleh ayahku)

Maka kalau dirubah menjadi kalimat aktif akan berbunyi: Ayahku akan membeli mobil antik itu

My father will buy that  antique car.

Contoh lain:

The new movie will be seen by us.

(Film baru itu akan ditonton oleh kami)

Maka kalimat aktifnya akan berbunyi

Kami akan menonton film baru itu.

We will see the new movie.

Semoga bermanfaat

27. Antique, vintage, retro, and classic, what does it all mean?


Antique=>An object can be called an antique if it is more than 100 years old, not a factory production or a mass-made product

Vintage=>Vintage items are usually categorized for objects that were made more than 20 years ago or are still under 100 years old. Generally, the design of vintage goods has its own peculiarities that represent a certain period when the item appears .

retro=>If antique or vintage objects come from past objects, then retro objects are categorized for objects that have just been made now but with ancient styles and designs

classic => old item but still good and valuable
