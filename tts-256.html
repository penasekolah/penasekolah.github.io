3. Opsinya :A) 175 / 256B) 162 / 256C) 122 / 256D) 81 / 256E) 108 / 256​


distribusi binomial

n percobaan → n = 4 turis

x sukses

p peluang sukses = 25% = 1/4 (kurang puas)

q peluang gagal = 1 - p = 3/4

P(x,n) = nCx . p^x . q^(n - x)

peluang paling sedikit 1 turis menyatakan kurang puas → x ≥ 1

= 4C1 . (1/4)¹ . (3/4)³ + 4C2 . (1/4)² . (3/4)² + 4C3 . (1/4)³ . (3/4)¹ + 4C4 . (1/4)⁴ . (3/4)⁰

= 4 . 3³/4⁴ + 6 . 3²/4⁴ + 4 . 3/4⁴ + 1/4⁴

= (108 + 54 + 12 + 1)/4⁴

= 175/256
