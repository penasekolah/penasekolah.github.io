4. there are some mint chocolates and dark chocolates in a bag. the number of mint chocolatos is 25% of the number of dark chocolates. what percentage of the chocolates is mint?


mint = x
dark = y

x = 25% y
x = 25/100 y
x = 1/4 y
4x = y
y = 4x

[tex] \frac{x}{x + y} \times 100\% \\ \\ = \frac{x}{x + 4x} \times 100\% \\ \\ = \frac{x}{5x} \times 100\% \\ \\ = \frac{1}{5} \times 100\% \\ \\ = 20\%[/tex]
