Pembahasan :

1. Diketahui :

L0 = 2 m

to = 20°c

α = 0,000011/°c

t = 100°c

Ditanya :

L = ?

Jawab :

∆t = (100 - 20)°c = 80°c

L = L0 (1 + α . ∆t)

L = 2 (1 + 0,000011/°c . 80°c)

L = 2 (1 + 0,00088/°c)

L = 2,00176 m ✔

2. Diketahui :

to = 25°c

L0 = 2 m

α = 0,000017/°c

t = 45°c

Ditanya :

L = ?

Jawab :

∆t = (45 - 25)°c = 20°c

L = L0 (1 + α . ∆t)

L = 2 (1 + 0,000017/°c . 20°c)

L = 2 (1 + 0,00034/°c)

L = 2,00068 m ✔
