Habitat: Cats are found in a variety of habitats, including homes as pets, on farms, and in the wild. They are adaptable animals and can thrive in both urban and rural environments.

Activity: Cats are active animals that enjoy hunting, playing and grooming. They are also known for their agility and climbing abilities. They are crepuscular animals, which means that they are most active during the dawn and dusk hours.

Characteristics: Cats are known for their soft fur, pointed ears and retractable claws. They have excellent night vision and a keen sense of smell. They are also known for their independent nature and tendency to be solitary creatures.

Wild cats at live in a variety of habitats from savannas to boreal forests. While domestic cats live in a house. Cats generally eat meat especially the domestic ones, the food needs to be cooked or else it could make them sick. Cats spend majority of their time walking, running and playing. They also enjoy sleeping. Cats are friendly and playful animals, they like being close to human.

Pembahasan

Untuk mengidentifikasi binatang biasanya kita menggunakan beberapa informasi secara umum seperti activity, habitat, food dan characteristic. Berikut adalah penjelasannya.

Activity is the condition in which things are happening or being done. Jadi, activity pada dasarnya adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan oleh binatang tersebut.

Habitat is the natural home or environment of an animal, plant or other organism. Sehingga habitat adalah suatu tempat atau lokasi alami dari suatu binatang, tumbuhan atau organisme.

Food is any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorbs in order to maintain life and growth. Sederhananya, food adalah makanan yang dikonsumsi oleh manusia, binatang, atau yang diserap tumbuhan agar dapat bertumbuh dan bertahan hidup.
