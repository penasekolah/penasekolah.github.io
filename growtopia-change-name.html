5. my name is written by me in All my bookschange to active voice​


Jawaban

Passive: My name is written by me in all my books

Active: I write my name in all my books

Penjelasan

Soal terkait active voice (kalimat aktif)

Active voice (kalimat aktif) adalah kalimat di mana subjeknya berperan sebagai pelaku aksi.

Pola Umum Kalimat Aktif:

S + V + O

Contoh:

Passive: Houses are sold by me every yearActive: I sell houses every year

Bisa dilihat pelaku aksi me pada kalimat pasif berubah menjadi I sebagai subjek kalimat aktif dan melakukan aksi sell terhadap objek houses.

Pembahasan Jawaban

Simple Present

Affirmative  

Passive: S + is/am/are + V3 Active: S + V1 + O

My name is written by me in all my books

⤷ I write my name in all my books
