9. General classification Buffalo​


Jawaban:

The classification of buffalo is still uncertain, but Bubalus bubalis is usually classified into three sub-species: Wild buffalo (B. bubalis arnee), the ancestor of the river buffalo. River buffalo (B. bubalis bubalis) who came from South Asia. The swamp buffalo (B. bubalis carabauesis) is native to Southeast Asia.

Jawaban:

Family Bovidae Gray, 1821 – antelopes, cattle, goats, sheep, bovids

Subfamily Bovinae Gray, 1821

Genus Bubalus C. H. Smith, 1827 – water buffalos

Species Bubalus bubalis (Linnaeus, 1758) – water buffalo, water buffalo (feral), Water Buffalo, domestic water buffalo
