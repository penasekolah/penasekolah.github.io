1. Buatlah dialog percakapan bahasa inggris 2 orang tentang kegiatan sehari-hari


Kiana was taking a morning walk until she saw her friend Seele.

Kiana; Good morning, seele!

Seele; Good morning, How are you doing?

Kiana; I'm doing fine, what about you?

Seele; I'm doing fine aswell!

Kiana; What do you do in daily activities?

Seele; That's a sudden surprise, but honestly i don't do that much. I usually help my mother in the kitchen after school!

Kiana; What do you usually do there?

Seele; I help her wash the dishes and cook food!

Kiana; What time do you come from your kitchen?

Seele; At 4PM.

Kiana; You're so nice and helpful, seele.

Seele; I appreciate the compliment, Kiana!

Kiana; Anyway, do you usually get up early at the morning?

Seele; Sometimes, waking up in the morning makes me feels fresh! :)

Kiana; What time do you usually get up?

Seele; At 4:30AM!

Kiana; What do you do after getting up?

Seele; I take a shower, and get dress.

Kiana; Do you always have breakfast?

Seele; Of course, i couldn't miss a breakfast.

Kiana; What time do you go to school?

Seele; Hmm, at 06:15AM! I don't want to be late!

Kiana; How do you go to school?

Seele; I go to school by walking, because my home isn't that far from school.

Kiana; Oh, i go to school by taking a car with my mother, because my house is pretty far from school. Anyway it was nice talking to you, Seele!

Seele; It was nice talking to you too, Kiana!
