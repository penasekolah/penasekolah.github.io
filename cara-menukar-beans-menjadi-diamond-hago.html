8. bagaimana cara menukar sebuah kalimat ke kalimat passive


Kalimat aktif: I clean my room every day. [Saya membersihkan kamarku setiap hari.]

Kalimat pasif: My room is cleaned by me every day. [Kamarku dibersihkan olehku setiap hari.]

Rumus kalimat pasif
Rumus kalimat aktif adalah S + auxilary verb + verb 3. Auxilary verb ini dapat berupa to be (is, am, are, was, dan were), have, atau modal bergantung pada struktur kalimat aktif dan subjek kalimat pasif.

19. *Twenty-five percent of his crop ...* A. Is soy beans B. Are soy beans C. Has soy beans D. Had soy beans


Subject : English
Category : Concord (Subject Verb Agreement)

"percent" is a collective noun. It can be either singular or plural, depending on the noun that follows the 'of'.
If the noun is singular then a singular verb is used.
If the noun is plural then a plural verb is used.

Example :
Ten percent of the money is gone.
Ten percent of the students are absent.

ANSWER :
Twenty-five percent of his crop is soy beans. (A)

*note : have (pilihan C dan D) tidak cocok karena artinya mempunyai soy beans.
