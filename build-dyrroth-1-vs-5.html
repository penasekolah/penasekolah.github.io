[tex] \it = (5 \times 5 \times 5 \times 5) \div (5 \times 5)[/tex]

[tex] \it = 625 \div 25[/tex]

5⁴ ÷ 5²

= ( 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 ) ÷ ( 5 × 5 )

= ( 25 × 5 × 5 ) ÷ ( 25 )

= 625 ÷ 25
