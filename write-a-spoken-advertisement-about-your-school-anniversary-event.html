Answer the following questions clearly and correctly!1) an advertisement has these four characteristics.what are they?2)what is the definition of event advertisement?3)what are usually mentioned in event advertisement?4)mention three verbs to advertise a charity event!5)write a spoken advertisement about your school anniversy event!tolong bantu ka...sekarang di kumpulkan​


An advertisement has these four characteristics, they are persuasive, creative, original, and promotional. The definition of event advertisement is an advertisement which is made to announce some events or activities whether it's free or a commercial purpose. Things are usually mentioned in event advertisement: the time and date of the events, the place, and the ticket price if it is commercially event. Three verbs to advertise a charity event are attend, give, help. A spoken advertisement about school anniversary event: "In commemoration of the 20th anniversary of SMA Negeri Satu, we will hold an open campus for the public which will be held on campus from January 19, 2021 to January 23, 2021. This event will also be enlivened by death metal and keroncong music concerts on Saturday, January 25, 2021. It's free! So be there, don't miss it!Pembahasan

Soal di atas adalah tentang advertisement atau iklan. Iklan adalah pengumuman dalam bentuk tulisan atau media lainnya untuk mengumumkan sesuatu, baik sosial atau komersial. Dalam soal di atas ditanyakan tentang advertisement untuk event atau acara kegiatan tertentu. Yang harus ditampilkan terutama adalah:

Waktu dan tanggal atau time and date kegiatan tersebut.Tempat kegiatan atau place, venue. Informasi tambahan berapa harga tiket atau gratis. Kontak yang bisa dihubungi untuk menanyakan informasi. Pelajari lebih lanjut
