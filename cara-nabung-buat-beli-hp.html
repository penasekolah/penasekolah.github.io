28. nabung 5 bulan dpt bunga rp4500. Awal nabung rp 120.000. Suku bunga tabungan?​


Mo x b% x n/12 = B

[tex]120.000 \times \frac{x}{100} \times \frac{5}{12} = 4.500 \\ 1.200 \times \frac{5}{12} \times x = 4.500 \\ 12 \times \frac{5}{12} \times x = 45\\ 5 \times x = 45 \\ x = 9[/tex]

jadi besar bunganya adalah 9%

maaf bila ada kesalahan

B = M × p/100 × b/12

4500 = 120.000 × p/100 × 5/12

4500 = 10000 × p/100 × 5

4500 = 100 × p × 5

4500 = 500p

4500/500 = p

9% = p

Jadi suku bunganya 9%
