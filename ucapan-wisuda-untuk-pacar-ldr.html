Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

4! + 5!

4!

= 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

= 12 × 2 × 1

= 24 × 1

= 24

5!

= 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

= 20 × 3 × 2 × 1

= 60 × 2 × 1

= 120 × 1

= 120

4! + 5!

= (4×3×2×1) + (5×4×3×2×1)

= 24 + 120
