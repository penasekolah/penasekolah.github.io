1. Frekuensi gelombang sebesar 200 Hz

2. Panjang gelombang sebesar 5 meter

3. Cepat rambat gelombang sebesar 330 m/s

[tex]\texttt{ }[/tex]

PEMBAHASAN

Marilah kita ingat kembali rumus gelombang berjalan berikut ini:

[tex]\boxed{v = \lambda f}[/tex]

dimana :

v = cepat rambat gelombang ( m/s )

λ = panjang gelombang ( m )

f = frekuensi gelombang ( Hz )

Mari kita sekarang mencoba menyelesaikan soalnya.

[tex]\texttt{ }[/tex]

Soal No. 1:

Diketahui:

cepat rambat gelombang = v = 150 m/s

panjang gelombang = λ = 0,75 m

Ditanyakan:

frekuensi gelombang = f = ?

Penyelesaian:

Kita bisa mencari frekuensi gelombang dengan rumus:

[tex]v = \lambda f[/tex]

[tex]f = v \div \lambda[/tex]

[tex]f = 150 \div 0,75[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{f = 200 \texttt{ Hz}}[/tex]

[tex]\texttt{ }[/tex]

Soal No. 2:

Diketahui:

cepat rambat gelombang = v = 1500 m/s

frekuensi gelombang = f = 300 Hz

Ditanyakan:

panjang gelombang = λ = ?

Penyelesaian:

Kita bisa mencari panjang gelombang dengan rumus:

[tex]v = \lambda f[/tex]

[tex]\lambda = v \div f[/tex]

[tex]\lambda = 1500 \div 300[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\lambda = 5 \texttt{ meter}}[/tex]

[tex]\texttt{ }[/tex]

Soal No. 3:

Diketahui:

frekuensi gelombang = f = 440 Hz

panjang gelombang = λ = 75 cm = 0,75 meter

Ditanyakan:

cepat rambat gelombang = v = ?

Penyelesaian:

Kita bisa mencari cepat rambat gelombang dengan rumus:

[tex]v = \lambda f[/tex]

[tex]v = 0,75 \times 440[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{v = 330 \texttt{ m/s}}[/tex]

[tex]\texttt{ }[/tex]

6. ipa kls 9 halaman 126​


Jawaban:

d.10 m/s^2

Penjelasan:

DIK :

F1 = 30N

F2 = 20N

F3 = 40N

m = 20kg

t = 4s

DIT : Vt (kecepatan akhir)?

Pembahasan

Catatan : F2 berlawanan arah maka menjadi negatif (-)

ΣF = F1 - F2 + F3

= 30N - 20N - 40N

= 50N

F = m.a

a = F/m

a = 50/20

a = 2.5 m/s²

Vo = 0m/s²

Vt = Vo + a.t

Vt = 0 + 2.5 × 4

Vt = 0 + 10

Vt = 10m/s

Jawaban yang tepat adalah 10m/s.

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

10. (+) My father was in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office  yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office  yesterday afternoon?
