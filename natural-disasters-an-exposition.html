2. cerita natural disasters


Natural Disasters
There are many types of natural disaster. They include earthquakes, floods, volcanic eruptions, drought, typhoons, tsunamis, hurricanes, forest fires, landslides, and my others. We may be able to prevent some natural Disasters from happening, such as floods and forest fires, but some can't be avoided.

There are some things we can do to prevent natural disasters such as forest fires, flooding, or landslides from happening. We can prevent forest fires by not being careless with fire. Many forest fires happen because people carelessly throw cigarette busts. Be careful with fire when you go camping or hiking. Make sure you put the fire completely out before you leave.

Floods and landslides can be avoided by not cutting down trees. The trees are supposed to hold water when the rain comes. If there are no trees, the water will just wash away everything in its path.

When a natural disaster happens, it may cause many casualties. People are killed or injured, buildings are destroyed, and lots of people lose their homes and other property.
