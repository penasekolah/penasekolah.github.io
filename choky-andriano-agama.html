Penjelasan:

1. Dik : t = 10 menit = 600 sekon

s = 6km = 6000 m

dit : v?

jb :

v = s*t

= 6000m * 600s

= 3.600.000m/s

2. dik : v1 = 8m/s

v2 = 12 m/s

t =2 s

dit : a?

jb :

a = Δv /Δt

a = v2-v1 /t2-t1

a = 12 m/s - 8m/s / 2s-0s

a = 4 m/s /2 s

a = 2 m/s^2
