9. Tentukan Series of events Ada 6The end of the story Ada 2Mau dikumpul besok​


Soal:

Tentukan

A. Series of events : Ada 6

B. The end of the story : Ada 2

Events (tahapan peristiwa) :

1. A week ago, I could not go to school. I couldn't go because I bruised my face

(Paragraph pertama)

2. On Friday morning, I rode my bike to school with Ed. I took my bag and water bottle.

(Paragraph kedua)

3. Some minutes later, I rode along slippery street. It was slippery after the rain. I was fine. I went through the slippery road.

(Paragraph kedua)

4. After that, I went along the bumpy road. Suddenly, I lost my grip and fell off my bike. I tumbled out my bike. The ground rolled. I was dizzy.

(Paragraph kedua)

5. After that, there was blood running from my cheek. My face was bleeding! I was startled. And the cut was hurt.

(Paragraph kedua)

6. My mum took me to the doctor's office. The doctor cleaned the cut on my face. He said that I should take a rest for a while. He also told me that I should wear a helmet when riding my bike.

(Paragraph ketiga)

The End of The story (Akhir dari setiap cerita) :

1. Luckily, Ed was behind me. He helped me stand up and took me home.

(Akhir cerita dari Paragraph kedua)

2. From that day, I do not forget to wear a helmet when I ride my bike.

(Akhir cerita dari Paragraph ketiga)
