Female gecko is ready to reproduce at the age of one year. But the male Gecko at the age of seven months is ready to mate. signs of the female Gecko ready to mate / ovulation (signs that can be recognized on the body of the female Gecko), can be seen in the reddened abdomen and very clearly visible eggs that may still be stored in her uterus.

how to marry a Gecko is done by uniting the two Gecko in one cage. It would be very difficult to pay attention to gestures or marital proceedings, because Gecko tends to prefer shade. then the custom Gecko is in the houses.

The characteristics of Gecko having been married can be seen in the abdomen of the female, in the groin of the hind legs close to the genitals and the female tail is very clearly seen the egg from the fertilization it does.

in one process of fertilization or marriage by Gecko, continued Sony, can produce six offspring. The laying process is gradual. Every 2-3 weeks the Gecko returns to lay eggs and that happens up to three times in one fertilization. then in the second marriage the graph or number of offspring produced will be doubled from the first marriage. for example in the first marriage the number of offspring numbered six, then in the second marriage the number will increase to 12 and so on .

Signs that a female Gecko wants to lay eggs after mating, can be seen on her stomach with eggs, and then it is recommended to continue to be fed. Because if a female Gecko wants to lay her eggs or lay eggs, she won't eat.

because Gecko tends to prefer shade, Sony also suggests, as long as the female will lay eggs, the female cage should be given a cocopeat base or fibers from coconut that have been soaked in water so that the female cage becomes moist and also issued eggs can be maintained.

the eggs produced by Gecko should be taken and moved to another place that is more comfortable. Place the eggs in a container containing Vermiculite, which is a layer of silica minerals that have undergone a heating process at high temperatures. vermiculite is commonly used as a planting medium, for certain types of plants, and can be used to help the process of incubation in reptile eggs, because it can keep moisture more stable. Vermiculite can be obtained at places that sell growing media, or in pet shops. then, place it in a place with a temperature of 27-32 ° C until the Gecko child is born.
