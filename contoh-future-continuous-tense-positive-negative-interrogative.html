1. Write an example of future continuous tense positive, negative, and interrogative!


FUTURE CONTINUOUS

Answer :

(+) Dew will be sun-bathing next month during the New year breaks.

(-)  Dew won't be sun-bathing next month during the New year breaks

(?) Will Dew be sun-bathing next month during the New year breaks?

Explanation

Future Continuous tense atau disebut juga Future progressive tense merupakan  verb tense yang merujuk penjabaran kejadian yang akan sedang di lakukan/ diharapkan / dibayangkan pada masa tertentu mendatang. Kejadian tersebut masih berlangsung dan belum diselesaikan juga pada saat itu.

Keterangan waktu yang digunakan, meliputi tomorrow morning, this time tomorrow, this time next week, next Sunday, tonight and etc.

1. VERBAL FUTURE CONTINUOUS

Dalam struktur verba, predikatnya terdiri dari pengikat subyek dan diikuti kata kerja utama.

Patterns :

(+) S+will+be+verb-ing + Complement

(-) S+will+not+be+verb-ing + Complement

(?) Will+S+be+verb-ing + Complement?

Example :

(+) She will be writing some articles this time tomorrow.

(-) She won't be writing some articles this time tomorrow

(?) Will she be watching some articles this time tomorrow?

2. NOMINAL FUTURE CONTINUOUS

Kalimat nomina, kalimat yang predikatnya terdiri dari pengikat subyek dan diikuti dengan kata benda (noun) / kata sifat (adjective) / keterangan (adverb).

Patterns :

(+) S+will+be+ Noun/Adjective/Adverb

(-)  S+won't+be+Noun/Adjective/Adverb

(?) Will+S+be+Noun/Adjective/Adverb?

Example :

(+) He will be at the beach this time tomorrow morning

(-)  He won't be at the beach this time tomorrow morning

(?) Will he be at the beach this time tomorrow morning?

3. Buatlah 5 kalimat Future Continuous tense Positive dan negative.​


Jawaban:

Future continuous tense adalah sebuah bentuk kata kerja yang menjelaskan sebuah tindakan yang akan dilakukan atau kejadian yang akan terjadi pada waktu tertentu dimasa depan. Bentuk ini biasanya diikuti oleh kejadian atau tindakan lain yang masuk ditengah-tengah rentan waktu (durasi) dari future continuous tersebut.

Rumus dari Future Continuous Tense adalah sebagai berikut :

[+] S + will + be + Verb(ing)

[-] S + will + not + be +Verb(ing)

[?] Will + S + be + Verb(ing)

Kalimat "She will be waiting there" sudah termasuk kedalam kategori future continuous tense karena mengandung subjek (she), pattern utama future tense (will), auxiliary verb (be) dan bentuk kata kerja -ing (waiting), serta kata keterangan tambahan yang dalam kalimat ini adalah kata keterangan tempat (there).

Seperti yang sudah dijelaskan diatas, bentuk ini biasanya diikuti oleh kejadian atau tindakan lain yang masuk ditengah-tengah rentan waktu (durasi) dari future continuous tersebut. Contoh :

"I will be cooking a dinner meal when she comes back from her office"

Aku akan (sedang) memasak makan malam saat dia kembali dari kantornya.

Berikut adalah 6 contoh future continous dalam bentuk positive, negative dan interrogative sentence.

1. I will be going to the market when my mother arrives home. [+]

2. She will be studying at the library after class. [+]

3. He will not be waiting there if the roof has many holes like that. [-]

4. She will not be watching TV tonight. [-]

5. Will you be leaving tonight sir? [?]

6. Will we be climbing this weekend? [?]

Di bawah ini merupakan rumus dari tenses di atas!

Simple present tenseVerb (kata kerja) do dan does

Do dan does merupakan verb dengan kata dasar do. Does digunakan untuk singular subjects (He, She, It This, That, A person's name), sedangkan do untuk plural subjects (I, You, They, We, These, Those).

    2. Kalimat adjective (kata sifat)

[+] s + be + adjective

[-]  s + be + not + adjective

    3. Kalimat noun (kata benda)

[+] s + be + noun

[-]  s + be + not + noun

[?] be + s + noun + ?

Simple past tense

[+] s + v2 + o

[-]  s + was/were+ not + o

    s + did/had + not + v1

[?] was/were + s + o + ?

    did/had + s + v1 + ?

Future tense

[+] s + will/shall + v1 + o

    s + be going to + v1

[-]  s + will/shall + not + v1 + o

    s + be + not going to + v1 + o

[?] will/shall + s + v1 + o + ?

    be + s + going to + v1 + ?

Simple perfect tenseKalimat nomina (noun)

[+] s + have/has + been + complement

[-]  s + have/has + not + been + complement

[?] have/has + s + been + complement + ?

    2. Kalimat verba (verb)

[+] s + have/has + v3

[-]  s + have/has + noun + v3

[?] have/has + s + v3 + ?

Present continuous tense

[+] s + be + v-ing + o

[-]  s + be + not + v-ing + o

[?] be + s + v-ing + o + ?

25. buatlah 2 contoh kalimat simple present tense, continuous present tense, perfect present tense positive negative interrogative​


Kelas           : VIII

Pelajaran    : Bahasa Inggris

Kategori     : Tenses

Kata Kunci : Simple Present, Present Continuous, Present Perfect, Positive Negative Interrogative.

------------------------------------

1. Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan fakta, kebiasaan, dan keadaan umum yang terjadi pada saat ini.

Rumus:

Positive (+) : S + V1 (s/es) + O

Negative (-) : S + don't/doesn't + V1 + O

Interrogative (?) : Do/Does + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh:

1. (+) I go to school.

  (-) I don't go to school.

  (?) Do I go to school?

2. (+) He plays football in the backyard.

   (-) He doesn't play football in the backyard.

   (?) Does he play football in the backyard?

2. Present Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk membicarakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung.

Rumus:

Positive (+) : S + to be (am, is, are) + V-ing + O

Negative (-) : S + to be (am, is, are) + not + V-ing + O

Interrogative (?) : To be (am, is, are) + S + V-ing + O + ?

Contoh:

1. (+) I am going to school.

  (-) I am not going to school.

  (?) Am I going to school?

2. (+) He is playing football in the backyard.

   (-) He is not playing football in the backyard.

   (?) Is he playing football in the backyard?

3. Present Perfect Tense

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu aksi atau kejadian sudah selesai dan menekankan pada hasilnya.

Rumus:

Positive (+) : S + have/has + V3 + O

Negative (-) : S + haven't/hasn't + V3 + O

Interrogative (?) : Have/Has + S + V3 + O + ?

Contoh:

1. (+) I have gone to school.

  (-) I haven't gone to school.

  (?) Have I gone to school?

2. (+) He has played football in the backyard.

   (-) He hasn't played football in the backyard.

   (?) Has he played football in the backyard?
