5. Andrew deposited some money in the bank at the beginning of the year. At the end of one year, he earned an interest and had $3045 in the bank. This was 101.5% of the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year. a) How much did Andrew deposit in the bank at the beginning of the year? b) What was the amount of interest Andrew earned?​


Jawab:

a) Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

b) The amount of interest he earned was $45. The interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The amount of Andrew's money in the bank at the end of one year is 101.5% of the amount of money he deposited at the beginning of the year:

$3045 = 101.5% × m

(m = the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year)

$3045 = 101.5% × m

m = $3045 ÷ 101.5%

   = $3045 ÷ (101.5/100)

   = $3045 × 100/101.5

   = $3000

∴ Therefore, Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

The amount of interest he earned:

$3045 – $3000 = $45

Because this is the amount of interest in one year, we can say that the interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.

25. what is seen directly before the end of January and February cannot be seen at the beginning of the month​


What is seen directly before the end of January and February cannot be seen at the beginning of the month? It is letter R.

Pembahasan

Soal di atas adalah soal teka-teki. Untuk menjawab soal ini, kita harus mengetahui artinya terlebih dahulu.

What is seen directly before the end of January and February(= apa yang terlihat secara langsung sebelum akhir dari Januari dan February)

cannot be seen at the beginning of the month (= tidak dapat dilihat pada awal bulan)

Kata 'the end' menunjukkan huruf Y pada bulan January dan February. Berarti, 'before the end' adalah huruf R, karena letaknya sebelum huruf Y apabila dilihat dari awal.

"Cannot be seen at the beginning of the month" menunjukkan bahwa huruf R tidak ada bagian depan dari bulan January dan February.
