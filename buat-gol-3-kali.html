PELUANG BINOMIAL

P(X = x) = nCx . p^x . q^(n - x)

p = 3/4

q = 1 - p = 1 - 3/4 = 1/4

n = 3

a. P(X > 0) = P(X = 1) + P(X = 2) + P(X = 3)

= 3C1 . (3/4)¹ . (1/4)² + 3C2 . (3/4)² . (1/4)¹ + 3C3 . (3/4)³ . (1/4)^0

= 3(3/4)(1/16) + 3(9/16)(1/4) + 1(27/64)(1)

= 9/64 + 27/64 + 27/64

= 63/64

b. P(X < 3) = P(X = 0) + P(X = 1) + P(X = 2)

= 3C0 . (3/4)^0 . (1/4)³ + 3C1 . (3/4)¹ . (1/4)² + 3C2 . (3/4)² . (1/4)¹

= 1(1)(1/64) + 3(3/4)(1/16) + 3(9/16)(1/4)

= 1/64 + 9/64 + 27/64

= 37/64

c. P(X = 3)

= 3C3 . (3/4)³ . (1/4)^0

= 1(27/64)(1)

= 27/64
