16. Arrange these jumbled words into good sentences1. Win - i - chess - competition - the 2. you - can - chess - well -the - play ?3. are - we - hide and seek - playing4. kite - let's - play - field - the - in5. you - play marbles - read - to - are ?mhon bantuannya​


Jawaban:I win the chess competition can you play the chess well?we are playing hide and seekLet’s play the kite in the fieldare you ready to play marbles?

Pembahasan

1. Win - I - chess - competition - the

(+) S + Verb 1 (-s/es) + O

=> I win the chess competition

Saya menang kompetisi catur

2. you - can - chess - well -the - play ?

=> can you play the chess well?

(?) Can + S + Verb 1 + O?

Bisakah kamu main catur dengan baik?

3. are - we - hide and seek - playing

(+) S + tobe (is/am/are) + V-ing + O

=> we are playing hide and seek

Kami sedang bermain petal umpet

4. kite - let's - play - field - the - in

(+) Let + S + Verb 1 + O

=> Let’s play the kite in the field

Mari kita main layangan di lapangan

5. you - play marbles - ready - to - are ?

(?) tobe (is/am/are) + S + Complement?

=> are you ready to play marbles?

Apa kamu siap utk bermain kelereng?

26. John is playing a chess game against Peter. If the probability that John will win is 4/7 and the probability that Peter will win is 1/7 , find the probability that the chess game will end in a draw.


John probability + Peter probability

= 4/7 + 1/7 = 5/7

Draw probability

= 7/7 - 5/7 = 2/7

So the probability that the chess will end in a draw is 2/7
