d = 2 . r = 2 . 10 = 20 m
t = 2 menit = 2 . 60 = 120 s
n = 2 putaran

b.) v?
c.) v'?

jawab:
a.) Perpindahan
Perpindahan = diameter
s = d
s = 20 m

b.) Kecepatan rata-rata
v = s / t
v = 20 / 120
v = 1/6 m/s

c.) Kelajuan gerak wiki

v' = 2 . π . 10²
v' = 2 . π . 100
v' = 200π m/s
