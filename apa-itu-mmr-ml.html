23. Tentukan MMr dari CH3COOH dan Al2(SO4)3!bantu dong kaks​


Jawaban:

Mr CH3COOH = 2×ArC + 4×ArH + 2×ArO

= (2×12) + (4×1) + (2×16)

= 24 + 4 + 32

= 60

Mr Al2(SO4)3 = 2×ArAl + 3×ArS + 12×ArO

= (2×27) + (3×32) + (12×16)

= 54 + 96 + 192

= 342

*Note : ArC=12; ArH=1 ; ArO=16 ; ArAl=27 ; ArS=32

The leaflet is for parents who have children within the range of age of 13 month months to five years old.

3 How old are children when they receive the MMR vaccine? (2 answers)

The children will receive the MMR Vaccine when they are 13 months old and 4 or 5 years old (the second vaccine or booster).

4 Some children have symptoms after the vaccine. What are they?

The symptoms are:

FeverThe skin within the injection area is sore, swollen or red
