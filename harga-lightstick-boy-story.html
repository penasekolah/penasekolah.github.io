20. Change this story into passive voice.Yesterday acarhit a boy onthestreet. Awitness called an ambulanceand the emergency service took the boy to the hospital. Two doctors did a surgery to the boy. Finally, the local police arrested the unobeyed driver


Answer:

Yesterday, a boy was hit by a car on the street. An ambulance was called by a witness and the boy was taken by the emergency service to the hospital. The boy was done a surgery by two doctors. Finally, the unobeyed driver was arrested by the local police.

-> jawaban dicetak tebal merupakan perubahan dari penyampaian aktif ke pasif

Discussion:

Kalimat pasif adalah sebuah kumpulan kata dengan mendahului obyek bertukar tempat menjadi subyek dan sebaliknya, obyek berperan sebagai sesuatu diperlakukan oleh subyek. Pada kalimat pasif, terdapat perubahan dari kalimat aktifnya.

Agar dapat merubah kalimat aktif ke pasif, perlu diketahui bahwa terdapat:

perubahan verbaadanya penggunaan to beobyek mendahului subyek (obyek + verba + subyek)O + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + by — S + ...dengan to be diperhatikan dari obyeknya (is: he, she, it, name, object | am: I | are: you, they, we, names, objects)verba dari simple present diubah menjadi bentuk verba untuk pasif (past participle (V3))titik-titik dapat diisi sebagai sisa kalimat

Kalimat pada soal berupa bentuk simple past—umumnya teks naratif mesti diceritakan dalam pengungkapan peristiwa di masa lalu.

O + to be (was/were) + V3 + by — S + ...to be diperhatikan dari obyeknya (was: I, he, she, it, name, object | were: you, they, we, names, objects)

Explanation:

Sekarang kembali pada soal; untuk merubah kalimat aktif ke pasif dapat menjadi:

-> Yesterday (C) a car (S) hit (V2) a boy (O) on the street (C)

menjadi:

Yesterday, a boy (O) was hit (was + V3) by a car (S) on the street.

-> A witness (S) called (V2) an ambulance (O) and the emergency service (S) took (V2) the boy (O) to the hospital (C)

(terdapat kata hubung and menggabungkan kalimat terpisah)

menjadi:

An ambulance (O) was called (was + V3) by a witness (S) and the boy (O) was taken (was + V3) by the emergency service (S) to the hospital.

-> Two doctors (S) did (V2) a surgery to the boy (O)

(obyeknya adalah the boy bukan a surgery, kata itu merujuk ke sesuatu dikerjakan oleh subyek)

menjadi:

The boy was done a surgery by two doctors.

-> Finally, the local police (S) arrested (V2) the unobeyed driver (O)

menjadi:

Finally, the unobeyed driver was arrested by the local police.
