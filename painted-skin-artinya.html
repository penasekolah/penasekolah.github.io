22. Their house ___ every year. a. Been painted b. Was painted c. Is painted d. Is painting


Jawaban:

Their house is painted every year.

Arti dari jawaban di atas adalah "rumah mereka dicat setiap tahun".

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas adalah soal passive voice dengan menggunakan simple present tense. Ciri dari simple present tense dari soal di atas adalah adanya keterangan waktu "every year" (setiap tahun).

Passive simple present tense: object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by subject.

Kalimat pasif lengkap dari soal di atas adalah

Their house is painted [by them] every year.

Active simple present tense: They paint their house every year.
