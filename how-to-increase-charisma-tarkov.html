4. bro can u tell me how to use increase,increased,increasing&increase..And please give an examples..Tq..Hope you read this.


Increase itu verb 1 digunakan dalam present tense

Ex: I increase my ability

Increased itu verb 2 digunakan dalam past tense atau masa lampau

Ex: I increased my ability last month

Increasing itu verb+ing digunakan dalam present participle tense (sedang dilakukan) biasanya diawali to be (is,am,are)

Ex: I'm increasing my ability right now.
